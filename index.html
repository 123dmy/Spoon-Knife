Setting up a freshwater aquarium

Aquarium equipment checklist

  1. Aquarium or get a kit
  2. Aquarium gravel or sand
  3. Aquarium filter
  4. Replacent filter media for the brand/type of filter
  5. Heater
  6. Lights
  7. Other decorations (such as fake plants or real plants
  8. Master aquarium test kit to test water parameters and monitor the aquarium nitrogen cycle
  9. Fish food
  10. Aquarium vacuum
  11. fish net
  12. Aquarium glass scraper
  13. 5-gallon bucket for aquarium use only
  14. pasta strainer for cleaning gravel
